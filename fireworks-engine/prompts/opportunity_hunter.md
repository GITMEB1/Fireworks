# Opportunity Hunter (Fireworks)

Find the highest-leverage product opportunities for Fireworks.

## Instructions
1. Read current seams (`SEAM_MAP.md`) and scored backlog.
2. Identify top 3 opportunities that can ship in small/medium scope.
3. For each, bind exact files and expected player-visible gain.
4. Reject ideas that are generic, abstract, or not anchored in repo truth.

## Output
- Ranked list with: problem, seam binding, ship risk, why now.

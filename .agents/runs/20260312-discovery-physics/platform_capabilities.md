# Platform Capability Scan — Web 2025

| Capability | Browser Support | Potential Use in Fireworks |
|---|---|---|
| **OffscreenCanvas** | High (92%+) | Move particle physics and rendering to a Web Worker to prevent main-thread lag during Supernovas. |
| **Canvas Filters** | High (95%+) | Apply `blur()` or `brightness()` dynamically for bloom/glow effects on shell bursts without manual pixel manipulation. |
| **Web Audio API** | High (98%+) | Replace asset-based sounds with procedural `OscillatorNode` sounds (ascent whistle, explosion boom, charge hum). |
| **PointerEvents Pressure** | High (Mobile) | Map pressure to charge speed or explosion "prestige" level for mobile users with styluses or pressure-sensitive screens. |
| **AudioWorklet** | High (90%+) | Low-latency audio synthesis for 1:1 input/sound feedback during slingshot aiming. |
| **DeviceOrientation** | High (Mobile) | Tilt-to-aim physics: rockets veer left/right as the device is tilted. |
